KRISTINA CALUMPONG

Hi! I'm Kristina- Half White, Half Filipina. Born and raised in Kansas City, MO. Now living in Los Angeles, CA but still rockin' that midwest charm. Much older than I look, goofier than you'd expect, and sometimes I laugh too loud. Good at keeping others secrets, bad at keeping my own. Some have said that I never let the grass grow too much under my feet. If I'm not coding, I am in spanish class or at singing lesson. Or traveling. So far I've traveled to 20 countries.

This led me to taking classes at General Assembly and UCLA extension. Though it has been an incredibly challenging skill to crack, I can say I feel validated in my choice to pursue it.
